localtime¶

The localtime role:

  • 13 Aug 2024 10:00:00 +03:00: .

  • 13 Aug 2024 10:00:00 +03:00: .

  • 13 Aug 2024 10:00:00 +03:00 (D MMM HH:mm):

  • 13 Aug 2024 10:00:00 +03:00 (HH:mm z):

  • 13 Aug 2024 10:00:00 +03:00 (HH:mm zzz):

localtime2¶

The localtime2 role has a different hover text and is designed for showing the timezone without a date.

  • 13 Aug 2024 (zzz): Your detected timezone is

  • 13 Aug 2024 (z): Your detected timezone is
